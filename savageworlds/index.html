How to use this website

On this website you can save ideas and charactes for "Savage Worlds" and "Dungeons and Dragons". How you do this is you use the two links up top to go to where you store the ideas and characters. The "other uses" button takes you to where you store your ideas for, edges, hindrances, ect. the character button takes you to a page where you can go to store your characters. How you store your characters is you let this website use your camera to take a photo of your character that it can display on the screen.
